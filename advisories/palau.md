# Palau

**Level 1 Advisory**
**Date:** Tue, 06 Aug 2024
**Source:** [State.gov Link](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/traveladvisories/traveladvisories/palau-travel-advisory.html)

---

**_Reissued after periodic review without changes_**

Exercise normal precautions in Palau. 

Read the [country information page](https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/International-Travel-Country-Information-Pages/Palau.html) for additional information on travel to Palau. 

If you decide to travel to Palau:

  * Enroll in the [Smart Traveler Enrollment Program (STEP)](https://step.state.gov/step/) to receive Alerts and make it easier to locate you in an emergency.
  * Follow the Department of State on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/travelgov/) and [X/Twitter](https://twitter.com/travelgov).
  * Follow the Embassy on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/usembassykoror/).
  * Review the [Country Security Report](https://www.osac.gov/Content/Browse/Report?subContentTypes=Country%20Security%20Report) for Palau.
  * Visit the CDC page for the latest [Travel Health Information](https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/destinations/list) related to your travel.
  * Prepare a contingency plan for emergency situations. Review the [Traveler's Checklist](https://travel.state.gov/content/passports/en/go/checklist.html).
