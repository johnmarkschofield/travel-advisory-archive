Exercise normal precaution

in **Slovenia**.

Slovenia is generally a safe destination for travelers.

Travelers should take standard precautions against petty crime, such as  pickpocketing and purse snatching. These crimes are common in tourist areas, city centers, and transportation hubs. Use caution at night.

  * Review our [Travel Guidance](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/slovenia.html#requirements "Slovenia Travel Guidance") for Slovenia to learn how to prepare for a safe trip.



Exercise caution in the **Julian Alps** : follow local guidance, check the [weather](/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/slovenia.html#weather "Slovenia Weather") closely, dress appropriately, and never hike alone.
