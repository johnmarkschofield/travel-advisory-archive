# São Tomé and Príncipe

**Level 2 Advisory**
**Date:** Wed, 08 Oct 2025
**Source:** [State.gov Link](https://travel.state.gov/content/tsg_aem/us/en/home/international-travel/travel-advisories/destination.stp.html)

---

Exercise increased caution

in **São Tomé and Príncipe** due to **health risks**.

Medical services in São Tomé and Príncipe are limited. There are no adequate trauma or ambulance services. Even minor health issues may require medical evacuation at the traveler’s expense. Review our information on [Travel Insurance](/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/insurance.html).
