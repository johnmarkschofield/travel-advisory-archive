# Equatorial Guinea

**Level 2 Advisory**
**Date:** Tue, 07 Oct 2025
**Source:** [State.gov Link](https://travel.state.gov/content/tsg_aem/us/en/home/international-travel/travel-advisories/destination.gnq.html)

---

Exercise increased caution

in**Equatorial Guinea** due to risk of **crime** , **health** , and **arbitrary enforcement of laws**.

**Crime  
**Petty crime is common throughout the country. Local police may lack the resources to respond effectively to reports of crime, even serious crime.

**Health  
**Medical services in Equatorial Guinea are limited. There are no adequate trauma or ambulance services. Even minor health issues may require medical evacuation at the travelerâ€™s expense. Be sure to bring enough prescription medications to last the entire duration of your trip. Make sure you have medical or travel insurance that includes medical evacuation. Review our information on [Travel Insurance](https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/insurance.html).

**Other  
**Travelers face the risk of arbitrary enforcement of local laws that may result in harassment and detention.
